every worrior has a name and a purpose..

The new era begins. Pick your clan and fulfill your destiny. Every Warrior has a name... and they are willing to die for you. Kenji, exiled heir to the Dragon throne. Shinja, leader of the the Serpents, consumed by greed and lust for power. Grayback, the Wolf clans chieftain and the strongest creature in the realms. And last but not least, Zymeth, the evil warlock who brings dread wherever he goes.

Battle Realms is a fantasy, real-time strategy innovation, inspired by kung-fu movies and Japanese mythology. Develop a village of peasants into a clan of highly specialized fantasy warriors, leading your clan through two possible scenarios, one heroic and one evil, towards your mythological destiny. Emphasizing real-time tactics over resource management, Battle Realms focuses on quick thinking rather than long-term planning.

about the game

Battle Realms is a real-time strategy video game published and released by Crave Entertainment and Ubisoft in November 2001. It was the first game created by Liquid Entertainment. An expansion pack Battle Realms: Winter of the Wolf was released in November 2002. In 2012, the game was re-released on GOG.com. In 2019, the game was re-released on Steam as Battle Realms: Zen Edition, in its early access state with functioning online multiplayer.
